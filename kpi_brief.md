## 3. Employee Utilization Rate
- **Definition:** Percentage of employees assigned to at least one project
- **Current Value:**
  - Total employees: 60
  - Unassigned employees: 18
  - Utilization Rate = (60 - 18) / 60 × 100 = **70%**
- **Interpretation:** 70% utilization suggests a solid workload distribution, but 30% idle workforce may indicate inefficiency.
